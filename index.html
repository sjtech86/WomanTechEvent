WHO
WE ARE

We are the London chapter of the Global WomenTechmakers program. We organise and host meetups, events and an annual International Women's Day conference focused on showcasing the work, talent and achievements of local women in technology.

OUR
ANNUAL CONFERENCE

Women Techmakers is Google's global program and brand for women in technology. As part of the global event series, we host an annual conference, in celebration of International Women's Day.

Talks from WTM London 2023 are available on our YouTube Channel- a full day conference where we heard from 16 fantastic speakers.
